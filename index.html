If given a combination of heads and tails. Checking this box will tell the application to pick the heads. Looping a Ritual




These inputs will calculate the ammount of mana you will have when chaining a ritual spell. For example 'Rite of Flame' has an input of 1 red and an output of 2 red. These inputs assume you have paid the mana cost for the initial cast alredy. Any subsiquent casts will use the mana outputted during the chain. The loop will continue until either the spell does not get returned to your hand, the 'How many times do you want to try' number of casts is reached, or you run out of mana to continue the chain. You can just leave these inputs empty if you're not trying to loop a ritual.
